## Production Notes

**Hooks that win:** price comparison receipts, "lifetime commission" disbelief face, news headline react, "I quit Uber" storytime, calculator whiteboard.

**Always include:** income disclaimer in caption or end card · affiliate disclosure if using your link · "Results vary" on earnings claims.

**Caption SEO template:** `[Primary keyword] · Fare Co-op [angle] · #rideshare #sidehustle #referral #farecoop #newrideshareapp`

**A/B test matrix:**
- Driver vs rider vs referrer protagonist
- Skeptic vs hype tone
- Local geo vs national trend
- Short (15s) vs tutorial (60s+)
- Face cam vs faceless vs screen record

**Landing page CTA pairing:** Each video → bio link → video on page → opt-in for playbook → email sequence with referral link.
